    Painting process at your property

    1. Spread drop cloths

    We cover the floors with drop cloths to protect them from paint drips and splatters. We also relocate small pieces of furniture and cover larger ones with plastic sheets.

    2. Switch plates

    We remove switch plates and outlet covers before the painting of the room to ensure that the finished paint job will have a clean look.

    3. Scrape off old paint flakes

    We scrape old paint flakes to make the surface nice and even.

    4. Fill in grooves, dents and holes

    We carefully inspect surfaces for cracks, holes, dents and other imperfections and use caulk or a lightweight spackling compound with a putty knife to fill and repair any holes or cracks.

    5. Sand surfaces

    Smoothing walls before painting is necessary for surfaces previously painted.

    6. Painting

    Roll the paint on the ceiling or wall in a zigzag pattern.

    7. Finish

    Once the painting is completed, we will walk through the painted areas to ensure any missed spots or any touch-ups are required.
